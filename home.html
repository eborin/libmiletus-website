LibMiletus: IoT device prototyping made easy!

The main goal of LibMiletus is to enable developers to easily design and implement IoT devices. To this end, the library has been designed to hide communication details so that the programmer can focus on the IoT device functionalities instead of the interoperability and hardware communication inner workings.

Get started now!

Arduino IDE ready

LibMiletus can be easily installed and used through the Arduino IDE, which makes it easy to use by programmers with little or no experience with embedded systems.

Miletus App

LibMiletus comes with a companion App for easy and direct communication between smartphones and IoT devices through WiFi and BLE interfaces.

MIT License

Anyone can modify and use the library for their own purposes!

Low-cost hardware

LibMiletus is compatible with low-cost hardware, such as NodeMCU and HM-10 BLE hardware.

Constrained hardware

LibMiletus is designed to be compatible with constrained hardware, hence, it works with ESP-8266 chips!
